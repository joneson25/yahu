Return To Sport Criteria

Minimum Criteria

  • Pain ≤2/10
  • ROM hampir penuh
  • No swelling selepas latihan
  • Strength ≥90%

Functional Criteria

  • Single leg balance stable
  • Hop test LSI ≥90%
  • Y-Balance asymmetry rendah
  • Landing mechanics baik

Sport Specific

  • Sprint / deceleration
  • Cutting / change of direction
  • Jump landing
  • Non-contact → contact → full play

Notes

Modaliti

Acute: ice, compression, elevation, protected loading. Subacute/chronic: heat before exercise, TENS untuk pain, ultrasound hanya jika sesuai dan bukan rawatan utama.

Progression Rule

Naik fasa bila pain tidak bertambah 24 jam, ROM meningkat, strength bertambah dan movement quality baik.

Safety

Rujuk doktor/fisio jika deformity, suspected fracture, neurological sign, severe swelling, night pain, fever, atau pain makin teruk.
